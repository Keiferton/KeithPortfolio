## Highlights
- Selected for the inaugural ChatGPT Lab cohort focused on early product testing and design research.
- Collaborated with product teams on learning-oriented features, including feedback on Study Mode and prompt behavior.
- Contributed a student use case that was later published in [**100 Chats for Studying, Career & Life**](https://chatgpt.com/100chats-project/).
- Led campus-facing events and conversations about responsible AI use in education and research.
- Proposed an SFSU x OpenAI campus ambassador concept that aligned with later CSU-wide ambassador efforts.

## Why it mattered
ChatGPT Lab gave me a chance to see how AI products are shaped before they reach broad use. I tested features in real academic workflows, focusing on where they actually worked and where they broke down in practice.

What made it valuable was the ability to give structured feedback grounded in real use, then see how those insights informed product decisions. It also gave me a clearer understanding of what makes a tool genuinely useful in a learning environment.
